## Partner With Us

WhatTheStack is the largest international developer conference in the region, held annually in Skopje, North Macedonia. With **800+ attendees**, **4 stages**, **30+ speakers**, and a full week of pre-event activities, it's the ultimate dev conference and the biggest tech event in the local dev community.

As a truly community-driven conference, WTS attracts everyone from experienced industry professionals, engineers, and designers to passionate students, companies, and IT communities pushing boundaries across the region.

## Community Partnerships

Are you a dev community, meetup group, or student organization? We have a dedicated community partnership track — no cost, just collaboration.

### What You Get

- **2 Free All-Access Passes** — Use them however you like (giveaway, team members, etc.). Need more? Let's talk.
- **10% Discount Code** — We'll set up a custom promo code (e.g., `YOURCOMMUNITY10`) so your members get 10% off their tickets.

### What We Ask

Help us spread the word:

- **Share the Event** — Post the event + your discount code in your newsletter, Discord, or Slack.
- **Spread the Word on Social** — Mention us on your social channels before the event (we'll provide a media kit).

Got other ideas for collaboration? We're all ears.
