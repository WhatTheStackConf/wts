## Partner With Us

Whether you're a startup looking to gain exposure, a company seeking to expand your reach or a fellow community builder looking to collaborate, we invite you to join us on this journey.

## Why Partner with WhatTheStack?

**WhatTheStack** is a community-driven event that brings together developers, designers, and virtually all other kinds of IT professionals. Whether you're looking to hire, promote a product, or simply connect with like-minded individuals, we offer a unique platform to achieve your goals.

## Reach a Diverse Audience

Partnering with us gives you direct access to a highly engaged community of tech professionals. From junior developers to seasoned architects, our attendees are passionate about learning, networking, and discovering new tools and technologies.

## Showcase Your Brand

Position your brand at the forefront of the tech industry. Whether through booth presence, raffles, giveaways, or branding opportunities, we offer various ways to increase your visibility and connect with your target audience.

## Support the Local Tech Ecosystem

By supporting WhatTheStack, you're investing in the growth and vibrancy of the local tech community. Help us create an environment where fresh ideas thrive and connections are made.

## Let's Collaborate

We are open to creative partnership ideas. Have something specific in mind? Let's talk about how we can work together to create an awesome experience for everyone.
