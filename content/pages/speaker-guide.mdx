## Travel, Accommodation, and Expenses

### Expenses

We will cover your travel and accommodation expenses in full unless your employer has agreed to cover them. If your employer is covering your expenses, we will be happy to recognize them as a conference supporter. Companies interested in further support can also see our [partnership options](/partnerships).

Keep the invoices and receipts from your reservations; we will share reimbursement instructions directly. Send us your itinerary once it is booked so we can coordinate the rest of your stay.

### Accommodation

We will coordinate your hotel booking with you. The hotel and standard booking window are **to be announced**. Send us your arrival and departure dates, whether you are traveling with someone, and any room or accessibility requirements as early as possible.

### Getting to Skopje

[Skopje International Airport (SKP)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/5sHodsHU3Y7GCmwSA) is about a 30-minute drive from the city center. Skopje is also reachable by bus from neighboring countries.

Send us your arrival and departure details once booked. Airport transfer arrangements will be confirmed closer to the conference. If you need a visa invitation letter or other travel documentation, tell us as early as possible.

### Food and Dietary Requirements

Food and drinks are provided throughout the conference day. Vegetarian and vegan options will be available. Tell us about allergies, dietary restrictions, or other requirements so we can plan for them.

### Pre-conference Activities

We are planning workshops, warmup events, and a community gathering before the main conference. The schedule is **to be announced**. If you would like to host a workshop, join another event, or spend extra time exploring Skopje, let us know.

### Bringing a Guest

If you would like to bring a +1, contact the organizing team and we will help arrange their conference ticket.

## Your Session

### Talk Details

Your title, abstract, key takeaways, biography, photo, and social links may be used on the website and in conference promotion. Please respond promptly when we ask you to confirm them. Talk ideas can evolve, but check with us before making a substantial change to the accepted topic.

WTS talks should give the audience useful ideas, honest experience, or practical takeaways. Mentioning a product you work on is fine when it supports the story; a product or sales pitch is not.

### Session Formats

Your acceptance and agenda slot will identify your format:

- **Main-stage session:** 35 minutes total. The usual split is 25 minutes for the talk and 10 minutes for Q&A, but you may use the full slot for the talk or choose another split.
- **Short-format session:** 15 minutes for the talk and 5 minutes for Q&A.
- **Workshop or special format:** We will confirm the length and setup with you directly.

Please finish within your assigned slot so the next speaker and the audience can move between sessions on time. If you are unsure which format you have, ask us before building your final deck.

### Recording

Conference presentations are recorded and published after the event on the [WhatTheStack YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@WhatTheStackConference). Contact us before the conference if your session contains material that creates a recording concern.

### Equipment and Demos

- Plan to present from your own laptop.
- The current presentation plan supports HDMI and USB-C connections, subject to final venue confirmation.
- Bring your laptop charger and any adapter you already trust.
- Tell us in advance about audio, network, live-demo, accessibility, or other technical requirements.
- Bring an offline copy of your slides and a backup plan for any network-dependent demo.

We will share the final room setup, slide handoff process, speaker check-in time, and on-stage support details when the venue plan is complete.

## Accessibility and Code of Conduct

Tell us about any accessibility requirement that would make your travel, hotel stay, preparation, or time on stage easier. We would rather plan early than improvise on the day.

Everyone taking part in WTS must follow the [Code of Conduct](/code-of-conduct). If you experience or witness a problem, contact an organizer or email [what@wts.rocks](mailto:what@wts.rocks).

## Helping Us Spread the Word

We appreciate speakers sharing their announcement and session details with their communities. We will provide promotional assets you can use. If you would like to record a short preview of your talk or collaborate on another piece of content, let us know.
