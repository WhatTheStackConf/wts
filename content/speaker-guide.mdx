## WTS 2026 Speaker Guide

Hi there!

We're excited to have you speaking at WhatTheStack 2026. Thank you for sharing your time and experience with the community. This guide covers the practical details for preparing your session and getting to Skopje.

Some practical arrangements differ by event: WTS coordinates the main conference, while each warmup community coordinates its own event. Your invitation or event host will confirm what applies to your visit.

This is a living document. We will update the items marked **to be announced** as the remaining plans are confirmed, and we will send speakers any time-sensitive details directly.

<h2 id="travel-accommodation-and-expenses">Travel, Accommodation, and Expenses</h2>

### Expenses

Travel and accommodation support depends on the event you are joining and the arrangement made with your event host.

If WTS invited you to speak at the main conference, we will cover your agreed travel and accommodation expenses unless you have chosen to cover some or all of them directly, or your employer has agreed to do so. If your employer is covering your expenses, we will be happy to recognize them as a conference supporter. Companies interested in further support can also see our [partnership options](/partnerships).

If a community invited you to speak at one of its warmup events, that community will confirm any travel or accommodation support available for your visit. WTS does not reimburse these expenses unless we have agreed this with you directly.

If WTS has agreed to reimburse you, email us your invoices and relevant booking documents together with your preferred method of reimbursement. Send your itinerary to the host coordinating your visit once it is booked. If you are unsure what applies to your visit, ask us or your community host before making a booking.

### Accommodation

If WTS is arranging your accommodation, we will coordinate your hotel booking with you. The hotel and standard booking window are **to be announced**. If you are arranging and paying for your stay separately, or your employer or community host is, ping us if you would like to stay at the same accommodation as other speakers and we will share the booking details. Send the host coordinating your stay your arrival and departure dates, whether you are traveling with someone, and any room or accessibility requirements as early as possible.

### Getting to Skopje

[Skopje International Airport (SKP)](https://maps.app.goo.gl/5sHodsHU3Y7GCmwSA) is about a 30-minute drive from the city center. Skopje is also reachable by bus from neighboring countries.

If airport transport is included in your arrangement, the host coordinating your visit will confirm the pickup details with you. Send that host your arrival and departure details once booked. If you need a visa invitation letter or other travel documentation, tell us and your event host as early as possible so we can establish who can provide it.

### Food and Dietary Requirements

Food and drinks are provided throughout the main conference day. Warmup event hosts will share the catering arrangements for their events directly. Vegetarian and vegan options will be available at WTS-organized events. Tell us and your event host about allergies, dietary restrictions, or other requirements so we can plan for them.

### Pre-conference Activities

The WTS 2026 programme begins with workshops, community-led warmup events, and a community gathering before the main conference. The schedule is **to be announced**. Your event host will confirm your session time, venue, and setup directly. If you would like to join another event or spend extra time exploring Skopje, let us know.

### Bringing a Guest

If you would like to bring a +1 to the main conference, contact the organizing team and we will help arrange their conference ticket. For a warmup event, ask the community hosting it about attendance and capacity.

<h2 id="explore-skopje">Explore Skopje</h2>

If you have time around the conference, these are good starting points for exploring the city:

- [Old Bazaar](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Old+Bazaar+Skopje) for historic streets, shops, cafes, and restaurants.
- [Stone Bridge](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Stone+Bridge+Skopje), the city-center landmark connecting Macedonia Square with the Old Bazaar.
- [Skopje Fortress (Kale)](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Skopje+Fortress+Kale) for city views and a short walk from the Old Bazaar.
- [Memorial House of Mother Teresa](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Memorial+House+of+Mother+Teresa+Skopje), on the pedestrian route through the city center.
- [Mount Vodno and the Millennium Cross](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Millennium+Cross+Vodno+Skopje) for trails and panoramic views over Skopje.
- [Matka Canyon](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Matka+Canyon+Skopje), just outside the city, for hiking, kayaking, and a longer break from the center.

<h2 id="your-session">Your Session</h2>

### Talk Details

Your title, abstract, key takeaways, biography, photo, and social links may be used on the website and in conference promotion. Please respond promptly when we ask you to confirm them. Talk ideas can evolve, but check with us before making a substantial change to the accepted topic.

WTS talks should give the audience useful ideas, honest experience, or practical takeaways. Mentioning a product you work on is fine when it supports the story; a product or sales pitch is not.

### Session Formats

Your acceptance and agenda slot will identify your format. You can use the total time as you see fit; the talk and Q&A splits below are suggestions:

- **Main-stage session:** 35 minutes total, with a suggested split of 25 minutes for the talk and 10 minutes for Q&A.
- **Short-format session:** 20 minutes total, with a suggested split of 15 minutes for the talk and 5 minutes for Q&A.
- **Workshop, warmup, or special format:** Your event host will confirm the length and setup with you directly.

Please finish within your assigned slot so the next speaker and the audience can move between sessions on time. If you are unsure which format you have, ask us before building your final deck.

### Recording

We record main conference sessions and publish them after the event on the [WhatTheStack YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@WhatTheStackConference). Warmup event hosts will confirm separately whether their sessions will be recorded and where recordings will be published.

### Equipment and Demos

- Plan to present from your own laptop.
- The presentation setup uses HDMI.
- We have a bunch of adapters for USB-C and other common connections, but feel free to bring your own.
- Bring your laptop charger.
- Tell us in advance about audio, network, live-demo, accessibility, or other technical requirements.

We will share the final presentation setup, slide handoff process, speaker check-in time, and on-stage support details closer to the conference.
